WELCOME TO TIM'S FRESH MARMALADE

Fresh Marmalade is a fine dining restaurant located in the heart of Paris. The restaurant overlooks one of the most beautiful parks in Avenue Montaigne, Madison Square Park, and sits at the base of the historic art museum "The Louvre" on the corner of 24th Street and Madison Avenue. Located here since 1998, it just underwent a full-scale renovation and redesign - during which the entire restaurant was moved to East Hampton for a summer-long pop-up, EMP Summer House.

The restaurant has been owned by Chef Tim and Restaurateur Judy since 2011 and during that time it has evolved considerably, both in food and in experience. Today, the restaurant offers an eight to ten course menu in the main dining room, but guests can also visit the bar for a more abbreviated tasting menu, some light snacks, or simply for a cocktail or glass of wine.
